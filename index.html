Meet Jonny

Standards

I am a USMC veteran who believes the small things usually tell you everything: being prepared, respecting someone's time, listening before getting started, and finishing the job the right way.

That is the same mindset I bring to barbering. Do you accept walk-ins?+

Yes, walk-ins are welcome when time is available. Booked appointments receive priority, so Booksy is the best way to guarantee a spot.

Do you cut children's hair?+

Yes. Children must be at least 4 years old and able to remain seated safely during the service.

Should I wash my hair before arriving?+

Please arrive with clean, sanitary hair and without heavy product buildup. This helps produce the best result and keeps the service on schedule.

Where are appointments located?+

Appointments are available in the Albuquerque area. The exact location will be provided to confirmed clients once the new location is finalized.

How do house calls work?+

House calls add $15 to the selected service within the standard travel area. Contact Jonny before booking if you are outside the area.

How long will my appointment take?+

Most haircuts take approximately 30-40 minutes. Designs, beard services, perms, and house calls may require additional time.
